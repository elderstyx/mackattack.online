About the Comic

17-year-old Mackenzie and her prodigious friend Agnes dare to investigate the nightmarish mysteries preparing to swallow their city. But whenever danger lurks, Mackenzie’s goosebumps erupt into spines, spikes, and claws that threaten to puncture her heart. When a ghost-hunting con-artist clues them in on an ancient and powerful demon that may be responsible, all three are pulled into a vast, dangerous conspiracy that could tear apart Mackenzie’s life in her final days - and undermine the free will of countless others.
